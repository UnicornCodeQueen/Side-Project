Section #4 : Important things and an introduction to BT21

BT21 is the first project of LINE FRIENDS CREATORS. It’s new IP created with the collaboration of LINE FRIENDS and BTS Rather than simply creating avatars of the physical appearance of artists, BT21 consists of 8 different charming characters created by BTS members, where all of them actively participated in the whole process from drawing sketches to elaboration.


BTS, the first artist to join the ‘FRIENDS CREATORS’ Project, is a creator that shows a connection in terms of the global character brand LINE FRIENDS’ keywords: Global, Millennials and Trending. BTS has a high popularity ranking and a strong influence in the world, and are great storytellers of our time who can share the story of ‘development in IP’. They send encouraging messages throughout the world and has gone viral internationally.


UNIVERSTAR BT21 is the fictional world of the BT21 characters, family and friends, following the story: Prince TATA from Planet BT, who had been traveling with VAN, crashed on Earth. In order to spread love, TATA decides to become a star. Finally, they have met six fascinating members: KOYA, RJ, SHOOKY, MANG, CHIMMY, and COOKY and together, they make ‘BT21’, and they are dreaming of becoming the most famous stars in the universe, THE UNIVERSTAR! Will BT21 become UNIVERSTAR, and spread love around?


Introduction to the characters

Exceptionally bright and talented KOYA. Sleeping cutie full of thoughts and thoughts of sleeping.

When dancing, MANG is cooler than ever. Veiled under mask, MANG's true identity remains a secret.

CHIMMY enjoys wearing a yellow hoodie, and is born with a passion to work hard on everything, no matter what.

Prince TATA from planet BT, has a super stretchy body as well as supernatural powers.

COOKY hopes to break out of the stereotypes from the cherubic appearance, and become tough.

VAN who seems to know everything about the world, protects BT21 24/7. #guardian


Trivia :

BT21 is an idea made by Si-Hyuk Bang after the album Love Yourself 'Her' remains in the top 20 of the Billboard 200 four weeks after its release. BT21’s eight cartoonish characters were inspired by drawings created by the seven BTS members, with one correlating to each member and the final one representing the band and its fandom, ARMY. BT21 has a game available on smartphones, which is named PUZZLE STAR BT21. The BT21 characters are gender-neutral. The fandom name for BT21 is UNISTAR
